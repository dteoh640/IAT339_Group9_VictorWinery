Victor Hill Estate, Okanagan

2016
Chardonnay

Our most popular 2016 Chardonnay is brimming with stone fruit, white blossoms and lovely notes of vanilla and clove. A gorgeous vintage in Okanagan, the wine is quite opulent, with a plush texture buoyed by white peach and honeysuckle notes. Despite its apparent richness, the wine has the trademark focus and lingering finish that our Victor Hill Estate is prized for.
